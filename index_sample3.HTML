Common Questions

Is this a dating app?
No! It’s a "connection" app. Most groups are mixed gender, and the focus is on community. Whether you find a best friend, a partner, or a gym buddy is up to the chemistry.
Who pays for the event?
Everyone pays for their own ticket to the activity (e.g., the museum entry fee). MixMatched organizes the logistics and builds the group for a small monthly fee.
What if I don't like my group?
That’s the beauty of the "Click Decision." If you didn't vibe, you simply select "No thanks" after the event. You never have to explain yourself to them.
